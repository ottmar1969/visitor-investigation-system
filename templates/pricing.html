‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a visitor identification?
Each unique visitor to your website counts as one identification. We track visitors across multiple sessions and provide comprehensive profiles including company information, contact details, and behavioral data.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. Unused identifications don't roll over to the next month.
What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?
If you exceed your monthly identification limit, tracking will pause until the next billing cycle or you can upgrade to a higher plan. We'll notify you when you reach 80% and 100% of your limit.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees! All plans include free setup and onboarding. We'll help you integrate the tracking code and configure your dashboard at no additional cost.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your service will continue until the end of your current billing period, and you'll retain access to your data for 90 days after cancellation.
